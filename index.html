The SDSU Computational Toxicology Laboratory is primarily interested in developing mathematical and computational tools to understand the effects of environmental pollutants on embryonic development. The lab is affiliated with the Computational Science Research Center, Department of Mathematics and Statistics and School of Public Health at San Diego State University.
